Frequently Asked Questions

Can I make special requests for my order?

Absolutely! We understand everyone has unique preferences. During the ordering process, you can use the customization options to add special requests or contact our customer support for more specific needs.

Are nutritional information and allergens listed on the menu?

Yes, we provide detailed nutritional information and highlight allergens on our menu. If you have specific dietary concerns, please check the menu or contact our staff for assistance.

How can I place an order for delivery or pickup?

You can place an order for delivery or pickup through our website or mobile app. Simply choose your items, customize your order, and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to select your preferred delivery method.

How can I track my delivery order?

Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a confirmation email or SMS with a tracking link. You can use this link to monitor the real-time status of your delivery.
